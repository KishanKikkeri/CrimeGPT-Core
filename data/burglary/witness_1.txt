WITNESS STATEMENT
Witness: Mrs. Kavitha Patel
Address: 12 Maple Street (adjacent property)
Date of Statement: June 14, 2026, 03:40 AM
Recorded by: Constable Arjun Mehta

I am the neighbor of Mr. Rajesh Iyer at 14 Maple Street. I was awake
at approximately 2:03 AM because my dog was barking. I looked out of
my bedroom window which faces Maple Street.

I observed a masked individual — dark clothing, appeared to be male,
medium build — crouching near the rear corner of Mr. Iyer's property.
I saw the person climb up and enter through what I now understand to
be the kitchen window. I did not see a vehicle.

I was frightened and did not call the police immediately. I waited
approximately five minutes to make sure I wasn't imagining things,
then I called emergency services at around 2:08 AM.

I have known Mr. Iyer for three years. He mentioned he would be
travelling to Hyderabad this week for a family function.

I do not know who this person was. I could not make out any facial
features. The lighting near the rear of the property is poor — there
is one street lamp at the far end of the alley.
