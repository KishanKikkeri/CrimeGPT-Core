INCIDENT REPORT — Officer Notes
Case: Residential Burglary, 14 Maple Street, Greenfields
Reporting Officer: Constable Arjun Mehta, Badge #2241
Date: June 14 2026 | Patrol Unit: Alpha-7

Received dispatch at approximately 02:26 AM regarding a possible break-in
at 14 Maple Street. Arrived on scene at 02:31 AM along with Constable Priya
Nair (Badge #2198).

On arrival, found the rear window of the ground floor (kitchen) had been
forced open. Glass fragments were on both the interior floor and exterior
sill. The occupant, Mr. Rajesh Iyer, was away (confirmed by neighbor). No
one inside the premises.

Entry point: rear kitchen window, ground floor. Window latch appears to
have been pried with a tool — visible scratch marks on the frame.

Items reported missing (per neighbor Mrs. Kavitha Patel who has a spare
key and did a walkthrough at our request):
  - One laptop (brand unknown, charcoal case)
  - One brown leather bag

A dark-colored bag was later found discarded near the rear alley
dumpster approximately 40 meters from the property. Believed to be the
stolen laptop bag. Item was photographed and collected. No further
processing conducted at scene.

CCTV: Property at 16 Maple Street has an external camera pointing toward
the rear alley. Owner (Mr. D. Krishnamurthy) was contacted and agreed to
provide footage. Officer Singh collected the CCTV drive at 03:15 AM and
transported it to the station evidence locker.

Fingerprint smudges observed on exterior of forced window frame.
Scene was photographed. Forensic team was NOT called to scene due to
resource constraints — decision made by shift supervisor.

Status at conclusion of patrol: Scene secured. Case forwarded for
investigation.
